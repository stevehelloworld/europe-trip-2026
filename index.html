第一次坐郵輪 × 太陽公主號

整理自公主郵輪官方 OceanReady／MedallionClass 與常見新手經驗，供行前與船上參考。

實際規定以公主郵輪 App、航海日誌與船上公告為準；以下為一般建議。

行前必做（出發前 1–2 週）

Before you go (1–2 weeks prior)

  • 完成公主郵輪 OceanReady® 線上報到（護照、緊急聯絡、健康聲明等）。
  • 下載 Princess App，熟悉艙房、餐廳預約與船上導航。
  • 列印或手機存好 行李吊牌 Luggage Tags；大件行李貼吊牌後才交給碼頭搬運。
  • 若可事先取得 Medallion®（智慧圓片）：當日佩戴登船；勿放託運箱。
  • 藥品、護照、貴重物、換洗衣物、充電器放隨身行李（大件可能傍晚才送進艙房）。
  • 確認簽證／入境：希臘申根、郵輪靠港、西班牙、土耳其 e-Visa 皆需齊全。
  • Complete OceanReady® online check-in (passport, emergency contacts, etc.).
  • Install the Princess App for dining, wayfinding and daily schedule.
  • Print / save luggage tags; attach before porters take large bags.
  • If you receive a Medallion® in advance, wear it to the terminal — never check it in luggage.
  • Keep meds, passports, valuables, a change of clothes and chargers in carry-on (checked bags may arrive late afternoon).
  • Visas: Schengen (Greece/Spain route) + Türkiye e-Visa for stopover.

Embarkation day timeline (01 Aug)

上午
Morning

市區輕行程；若太熱可提早往派里厄斯。先吃點東西，船上雖有午餐但人多。

Light Athens sights; head to Piraeus early if hot. Eat a little — ship lunch gets crowded.

≤ 15:00

Arrive Terminal B / Gate E12. Typical flow: bag drop → security → ID/photo → Medallion pickup/activation → board.

登船後 1–2 小時
First 1–2 hrs onboard

先找艙房（可能尚未能進房）→ 主餐廳或自助午餐 → 逛公共區（Piazza／The Dome 方向）→ 看 App 上的安全演練／Muster時間與地點，務必參加。

Find your stateroom (may not open yet) → lunch → explore public areas → complete the required muster / safety drill in the app/onboard — mandatory.

17:00

Your reserved MDR Deck 7 time: arrive on time; meet your waitstaff.

19:00

Sail-away: upper decks/aft for photos. Large luggage often arrives by early evening — unpack then.

晚上
Evening

看秀、熟悉 App 點餐／找人功能、設定次日鬧鐘（港日要早）。

Show, explore Medallion app features, set alarm for early port days.

Medallion® 新手重點（太陽公主號）

Medallion® basics (Sun Princess)

  • 房卡＋消費＋定位：碰門把進房；船上消費多記在艙房帳；App 可找同行者（需雙方同意設定）。
  • OceanReady 越早完成越好，碼頭報到較順。
  • 圓片可掛頸繩／手環（船上商店有配件）；遺失立刻櫃檯掛失。
  • 下船前帳務用信用卡結清；保留紙本／App 帳單截圖。
  • Key + wallet + wayfinding: opens stateroom; charges to folio; app can locate companions if enabled.
  • Finish OceanReady early for smoother terminal check-in.
  • Lanyards/bracelets available onboard; report a lost Medallion at Guest Services immediately.
  • Settle your onboard account before final disembarkation; keep a folio screenshot.

靠港日時間軸 — 建議節奏

Port day rhythm

到港前一晚
Night before

看航海日誌／App：到離港時間、All Aboard 登船截止、是否接駁船（Tender）。準備護照影本、現金歐元、帽子防曬、行動電源。

Check Patter/app: arrival/departure, all-aboard time, tender or dock. Prep passport copy, EUR cash, sun protection, power bank.

清晨
Early morning

港日建議早早餐。聖托里尼等 Tender 港：盡早領接駁序號，越早下船排隊纜車越短。

Breakfast early. On tender ports (e.g. Santorini): get tender tickets early to beat cable-car queues.

白天
Daytime

自由行請自己盯時間；船不會為個別旅客晚開。建議回船時間比 All Aboard 再早 60–90 分鐘（塞車／纜車）。

Independent tours: the ship will not wait. Aim to return 60–90 minutes before all-aboard.

回船後
Back onboard

洗手、補水、小睡或泳池。離港可到甲板看港景。晚上秀與主餐廳照表操課。

Hydrate, rest, pool. Watch sail-out. Evening shows & MDR as scheduled.

海上日 — 可以做什麼

Sea day ideas

  • 睡晚一點、主餐廳慢用早餐、英式下午茶（看當日公告）。
  • 泳池／The Dome、散步甲板、講座（港口導覽很實用）。
  • Spa 僅參觀可免費；療程與精品為額外付費。
  • 晚上：劇院大秀、廣場表演、酒吧；服裝多為 smart casual，主題晚可稍正式。
  • 用 App 看當日 Princess Patter 活動表，標記想去的 2–3 個即可，別排太滿。
  • Sleep in, leisurely MDR breakfast, afternoon tea if offered.
  • Pool / The Dome, deck walks, enrichment talks (port lectures help).
  • Spa tours may be free; treatments & shops cost extra.
  • Evening: theater, Piazza entertainment; usually smart casual.
  • Use the daily schedule — pick 2–3 highlights, don’t overbook.

下船日（08/08）— 建議流程

Disembarkation day (08 Aug)

前一晚
Night before

按船上指示：大件是否要前一晚放走廊、行李吊牌顏色／時段。結清艙房帳。護照與隨身行李自己留著。

Follow luggage instructions (tags/color/time). Settle folio. Keep passport & carry-on with you.

~06:00–09:00

吃完早餐後約 09:00 批次下船（可選較後批次較不趕）。在 Terminal D／E 行李廳提大件 → 包車赴飯店。

Breakfast, then disembark around 09:00 (later groups are calmer). Collect bags in terminal hall → transfer to hotel.

消費、小費、網路

Money, gratuities, Wi‑Fi

  • 多數 onboard 消費掛艙房帳；特色餐廳、酒類、Spa、岸上行程常為加價。
  • 服務費／小費（gratuities）常每日自動入帳（金額以你們購買的套餐或船上公告為準）；可到客服調整。
  • 船上 Wi‑Fi 多為付費方案；可用 App 離線功能與船內通訊。
  • 電壓多為 110V／歐規插座並存（視艙房），帶萬用轉接最保險。
  • 暈船：中下層靠中間艙房較穩；可備暈船藥，必要時詢問醫療中心。
  • Most purchases go to your folio; specialty dining, drinks, spa, excursions usually cost extra.
  • Daily gratuities are often auto-added (check your package/onboard notice); adjust at Guest Services if needed.
  • Wi‑Fi is typically paid; Medallion app still helps with onboard navigation.
  • Power: bring a universal adapter.
  • Seasickness: mid-ship lower decks are calmer; pack remedies; medical center available.
